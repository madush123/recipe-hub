Delicious Appetizer Recipes

1. Classic Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe **Avocados**, mashed
  • 1 small **Onion**, finely chopped
  • 1-2 **Jalapeño** peppers, seeds removed and minced (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons **Cilantro**, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon **Lime Juice** (fresh is best)
  • 1/2 teaspoon **Salt** (or to taste)
  • Pinch of **Black Pepper**
  • 1 **Tomato**, seeds removed and diced (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a medium bowl.
  2. **Mash** the avocado with a fork to your desired consistency (chunky or smooth).
  3. Stir in the chopped **onion**, **jalapeño** (if using), **cilantro**, and **lime juice**.
  4. Season with **salt** and **pepper**. Mix well.
  5. Gently fold in the diced **tomato** (if using).
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Serve immediately with tortilla chips.

2. Simple Bruschetta

Ingredients

  • 1 French **Baguette**, sliced into 1/2-inch thick pieces
  • 2 tablespoons **Olive Oil** (for brushing bread)
  • 3 medium **Tomatoes** (Roma or cherry), diced
  • 2 cloves **Garlic**, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh **Basil**, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon **Balsamic Vinegar**
  • 1 tablespoon **Olive Oil** (for topping mixture)
  • **Salt** and **Black Pepper** to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Brush both sides of the baguette slices with **olive oil** and arrange on a baking sheet.
  2. **Toast** in the oven for about 8-10 minutes, or until lightly golden and crisp. Remove and let cool.
  3. While the bread is toasting, prepare the topping: In a medium bowl, combine the diced **tomatoes**, minced **garlic**, chopped **basil**, **balsamic vinegar**, and 1 tablespoon of **olive oil**.
  4. Season the tomato mixture generously with **salt** and **pepper**. Stir well.
  5. Spoon a generous amount of the tomato mixture onto each toasted baguette slice.
  6. Serve immediately. A drizzle of extra balsamic glaze on top is optional.
