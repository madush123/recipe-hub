Sweet Dessert Recipes

1. No-Bake Peanut Butter Bars

Ingredients (The Base)

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) **Unsalted Butter**, melted
  • 2 cups **Graham Cracker Crumbs**
  • 2 cups **Powdered Sugar** (Confectioners' Sugar)
  • 1 cup **Peanut Butter** (creamy or crunchy)

Ingredients (The Topping)

  • 1 cup **Chocolate Chips** (semi-sweet or milk)
  • 1/2 cup **Peanut Butter**

Steps

  1. **Prepare the pan:** Line a 9x13 inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides.
  2. **Make the base:** In a large bowl, combine the melted **butter**, **graham cracker crumbs**, **powdered sugar**, and 1 cup of **peanut butter**. Mix until fully combined.
  3. **Press the base:** Press the mixture evenly and firmly into the prepared pan.
  4. **Make the topping:** In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the **chocolate chips** and the remaining 1/2 cup of **peanut butter**. Microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring between each, until completely smooth.
  5. **Pour the topping:** Pour the chocolate/peanut butter mixture over the base and spread evenly.
  6. **Chill:** Refrigerate for at least **2 hours** (or until set). Use the parchment paper overhang to lift the bars out of the pan, then slice and serve.

2. Simple Apple Crumble

Ingredients (The Filling)

  • 6 medium **Apples** (such as Granny Smith or Honeycrisp), peeled, cored, and diced
  • 1/2 cup **Granulated Sugar**
  • 1 tablespoon **All-Purpose Flour**
  • 1 teaspoon **Cinnamon**
  • 1 tablespoon **Lemon Juice**

Ingredients (The Topping)

  • 1 cup **All-Purpose Flour**
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) cold **Unsalted Butter**, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup packed **Brown Sugar**
  • 1/2 teaspoon **Cinnamon**
  • Pinch of **Salt**

Steps

  1. **Preheat** oven to 375°F (190°C). Lightly grease an 8x8 inch baking dish.
  2. **Prepare the filling:** In a large bowl, toss the diced **apples** with the **sugar**, 1 tablespoon of **flour**, 1 teaspoon of **cinnamon**, and **lemon juice**. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish.
  3. **Make the topping:** In a separate bowl, whisk together 1 cup of **flour**, **brown sugar**, 1/2 teaspoon of **cinnamon**, and **salt**.
  4. **Cut in the butter:** Use your fingers or a pastry blender to cut the cold **butter** cubes into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  5. **Assemble:** Sprinkle the crumble topping evenly over the apple filling.
  6. **Bake** for 35 to 45 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the apple filling is bubbling. Serve warm with ice cream or whipped cream.
